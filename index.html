Dragons Crown Runeodex
Find possible spells based on the runes carved in the world. Don't worry game doesn't require the runes to be clicked in order. Most mission based runes have very limited areas in which they can be cast and are slightly darker, you van brighten them back up by tapping or placing your cursor over them.

This has been a quick side project and not extensively tested for device compatibility it should however work on most smart phones, it is designed to work in portrait mode pretty much exclusively. The runeodex is open source, you can find it on github at pgooch/dragons-crown-runeodex.
